MISC. | PRINT DESIGN & THE COMPUTER, SPRING ‘11 | D.C. TWEED RIDE

Tweed Ride is an annual bike ride held in Washington, D.C. which encourages participants to ride throughout D.C. while dressed up in 1920's and 1930's clothing. The design of all of the materials, the save the date, invitation, pamphlet, and giveaway, all mirror the humorous but seemingly formal tone of the event.
